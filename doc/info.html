Archive of Laochengxiang is a project by Jay Li. The 3d scans in this project were captured between 2020 and 2021. They document the spatialtemporalities in the demolishing historic Shanghainese neighborhood Laochengxiang (老城厢) during the Covid-19 pandemic. The project website is designed Jay Li and developed by Siyu Chen.
